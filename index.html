About Me

My name is Nabeel Alghaith. I am attending North Carolina State University in Raleigh while achieving a B.S degree in Computer Science with a concentration in Game Development with a minor in Mathematics. Some courses that I have taken that have strengthened my skills are Operating Systems, Software Engineering, C and Software Tools, Software Development Fundamentals, Interactive Game Design, Game Engine Foundations, and Data Structures and Algorithms.

Outside of school, I like to play and create video games. Being in the video game industry has been a dream of mine since middle school. I like to attend game jams and view other people's work. I am also in the Video Game Development Club at my college where we attend workshops in different topics while also having mini game jams. I had the amazing opportunity to attend GDC 2024 with the club and it has inspired me to continue with game design.

Some other hobbies I enjoy are hanging out with friends, spontaneous trips, and relaxing at home while watching a TV show or movie either with friends and family or by myself. I also try to keep a workout routine when I have the time to.
